Adaptive Immune System

The term "immune system" refers to a group of cells, substances, and mechanisms that work to defend the skin, nasal passages, intestinal tract, and other organs against foreign antigens including viruses, cancerous cells, poisons, and microbes (organisms like bacteria, fungus, and parasites).
